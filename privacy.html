Privacy Policy - Vigour Seeds

Last updated: June 2026

Vigour Seeds ("we", "us") operates a WhatsApp-based assistant to help farmers and distributors with crop guidance, product information, and support.

Information We Collect

When you message us on WhatsApp, we may collect your WhatsApp phone number, name, location (state/district), crop and farming details you provide, and any images you send for crop diagnosis.

How We Use Your Information

We use this information only to respond to your queries, provide crop and product recommendations, connect you with nearby dealers, and improve our service.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared with our authorised dealers or agronomists solely to assist you.

Data Storage

Your information is stored securely and retained only as long as needed to provide our service.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by messaging us on WhatsApp.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact Vigour Seeds via WhatsApp.
